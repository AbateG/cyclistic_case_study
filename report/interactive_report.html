💡 Key Strategic Insights

🚴‍♂️ Member Behavior

Members take shorter, consistent rides (avg. 11.8 min in 2019, 10.7 min in 2020) primarily on weekdays, indicating commuter usage patterns.

🚲 Casual Patterns

Casual riders take longer trips (avg. 20.0 min in 2019, 16.9 min in 2020) concentrated on weekends, suggesting recreational use.

📈 Statistical Significance

All observed differences are statistically significant (p < 0.0001), confirming distinct user behaviors across both years.

💰 Revenue Opportunity

Converting casual riders to members could generate $2.8 million in additional annual revenue based on current pricing.
